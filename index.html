Point Collector

Score points with Shaq, Rodman, Big Baby, and Swaggy P!!!!!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four images of former NBA players below.
  3. Instead of scoring 1,2, or 3 points as they would in a game, you will add a specific random amount of points (1 to 12 per click) to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each player is hidden and unknown until you click on it.
  7. The player's value per click will change every time the game restarts. Have fun!
