Shooting Contest (with bad shooters)

Shaq, Lonzo, Ben Wallace, and Dennis Rodman are all great NBA players with unique individual talent, but they all share one common trait: they CAN'T shoot. Read the rules below to try and win the game with these dreadful shooters!


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four images of Shaq, Lonzo, Ben Wallace, and Dennis Rodman.
  3. Instead of scoring either 1,2, or 3 points as they would in a game, for each click on a certain player's image you will add a specific random amount of points (1 to 12 per click) to your total score.
  4. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  5. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. The value of each player's image is hidden and unknown until you click on it.
  7. Each player's image's value per click will change every time the game restarts. Have fun!
